The Protocol
How Ripple works
01
You open a track
Your Ripple client searches the distributed hash table for peers who have the audio data you need.
02
Peers are discovered
The closest, fastest peers respond in milliseconds. No central broker. No middleman. Just direct connections.
03
Data streams directly
Audio chunks flow directly from peer to you — encrypted, verified, and at full fidelity. You're also seeding for others.
